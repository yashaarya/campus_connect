About Campus Connect



Campus Connect is a platform created to help students quickly discover nearby sports facilities around their college including gyms, swimming pools, turfs, grounds, courts, and sports shops.


Our mission is simple: make it easier for students to stay active, find affordable places, compare options, and enjoy sports without wasting time searching.


Whether you're looking to join a gym, book a turf with friends, or explore new sports, Campus Connect has you covered.
